Charts are created in Explore, the chart builder.

To create a chart:

1. In the top navigation bar, open **Charts**.
2. Click the **+ Chart** button in the top-right corner.
3. On the **Create a new chart** page, pick a dataset from the **Choose a dataset** selector.
4. Pick a chart type from the gallery, for example **Line Chart**, **Bar Chart**, or **Table**.
5. Click **Create new chart** to open Explore.
6. In Explore, the left panel shows the dataset's columns and metrics. The **Data** tab in the middle panel holds the query controls, such as **Metrics** and **Dimensions**. Drag columns into these fields or click a field to configure it.
7. Click **Create chart** to run the query and render the result.
8. Click **Save** in the top-right corner, give the chart a name, and optionally add it to a dashboard in the same dialog.

The controls shown in the **Data** tab depend on the chart type you picked. Use the **View all charts** link on the chart-type gallery to browse every available visualization.
