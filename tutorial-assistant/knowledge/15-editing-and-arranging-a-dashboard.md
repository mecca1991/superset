topic: Editing and arranging a dashboard
routes:

---

Dashboards are rearranged in edit mode.

1. Open the dashboard and click **Edit dashboard** in the top-right corner.
2. Drag charts to reposition them; the layout snaps to a grid. Drag a chart's bottom-right corner to resize it.
3. The right-hand panel has two tabs:
   - **Charts** lists saved charts you can drag onto the canvas.
   - **Layout elements** provides structure: **Tabs**, **Row**, **Column**, **Header**, **Text**, and **Divider**. Drag these onto the canvas to organize sections; drop charts inside rows, columns, or tabs.
4. To remove a chart from the dashboard, hover over it and use the delete (trash) action in its corner. This removes it from the dashboard without deleting the saved chart.
5. Click **Save** to keep the layout, or **Discard** to abandon the changes.

Outside edit mode, the **⋮** menu in the top-right offers **Edit properties** (title, owners, color scheme, JSON metadata), **Set auto-refresh interval**, and **Download** options.

A **Text** element accepts Markdown, which is a simple way to add section descriptions or usage notes between charts.
