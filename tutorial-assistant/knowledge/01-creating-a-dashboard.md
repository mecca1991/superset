A dashboard is a collection of charts arranged on one page.

To create a dashboard:

1. In the top navigation bar, open **Dashboards**.
2. Click the **+ Dashboard** button in the top-right corner.
3. A new dashboard opens in edit mode with the placeholder title **[ untitled dashboard ]**. Click the title and type a name.
4. The right-hand panel lists your saved charts under the **Charts** tab. Drag a chart card from the panel onto the dashboard canvas.
5. Resize a chart by dragging its bottom-right corner. Move it by dragging the chart to a new position.
6. Click **Save** in the top-right corner to store the dashboard.

You need at least one saved chart before a dashboard can show data. If the chart list is empty, create a chart first in Explore and save it.

To open an existing dashboard, go to **Dashboards** and click its name in the list. To make further changes, open the dashboard and click **Edit dashboard**.
